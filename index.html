What is Battery Swapping?

Battery swapping is a method where a depleted battery is exchanged for a fully charged one in minutes.

EV battery swapping technology allows electric vehicle owners to quickly replace a depleted battery with a fully charged one at a designated station, rather than waiting for the battery to recharge. This method aims to reduce downtime associated with charging, making EV ownership more convenient and potentially faster than refueling a gasoline car.
